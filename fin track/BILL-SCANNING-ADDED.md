- **Home Screen**: Click the **"Scan"** button next to "New"
- **Add Transaction**: Click the **📷 Camera icon** in the form
- **Quick Add**: Use the camera button for instant scanning

### **2. Scan Process**
1. **Point camera** at receipt/bill
2. **Tap "Capture Photo"** or upload existing image
3. **Wait for OCR** processing (shows progress)
4. **Review extracted data** (amount, merchant, date, category)
5. **Click "Use This Data"** to pre-fill the form
6. **Edit if needed** and save transaction
