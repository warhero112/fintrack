## 🚀 **How to Use Goals:**

### **1. Access Goals Menu:**
- **Click "Goals"** in the bottom navigation
- **View all your financial goals**

### **2. Create a Goal:**
- **Click the "+" button** in the top right
- **Enter goal title** (e.g., "Emergency Fund")
- **Add description** (e.g., "Build 6 months of expenses")
- **Set target amount** (e.g., $10,000)
- **Set current amount** (e.g., $2,500)
- **Choose target date** (e.g., Dec 31, 2024)
- **Select category** (Savings, Debt, Investment, etc.)
- **Set priority** (Low, Medium, High)
- **Click "Add Goal"**

### **3. Track Progress:**
- **View progress bars** showing completion percentage
- **See days remaining** until target date
- **Click "Add Progress"** to add money to your goal
- **Edit or delete** goals as needed

---

## ⚙️ **How to Use Custom Categories:**

### **1. Access Settings:**
- **Click hamburger menu** (☰) in top left
- **Scroll down to "Custom Categories"**

### **2. Manage Income Categories:**
- **Click "+ Add Income Category"**
- **Type your category name** (e.g., "Side Hustle")
- **Press Enter** to save
- **Edit or delete** existing categories

### **3. Manage Expense Categories:**
- **Click "+ Add Expense Category"**
- **Type your category name** (e.g., "Coffee")
- **Press Enter** to save
- **Edit or delete** existing categories

### **4. Use in Transactions:**
- **When adding transactions**, categories will show your custom ones
- **Income transactions** show your custom income categories
- **Expense transactions** show your custom expense categories
